Basic campaign observing information: WR6 (aka HD 50896 / EZ CMa): This V~6.9, WN4-s star is near Sirius. (06 54 13.0, -23 55 42.0, epoch 2000)

HR 4468 (aka HD100889, tet Crt), V~4.7, B9.5V, could be used as a continuum standard (11 36 40.8, -09 48 08.1, epoch 2000). It will be used for the CTIO echelle survey.

  1. If your wavelength window is limited to the 100A to 150A range then then HeII 5411 is the primary central wavelength choice. If you have a wider window then our old friend HeII 4686 is the line of interest.
  2. A minimum resolution, R, of 5000 is required and a maximum of no more than 10,000 is needed.
  3. A SNR of at least 100 is required.

The project PI is Anthony Moffat
