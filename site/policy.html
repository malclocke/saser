SASER data usage policy

SASER exists as a communication front between professional and amateur spectroscopists interested in southern hemisphere objects. Data published on this page are reduced by the observers, and shared with all SASER subscribers regularly in order to validate the observations.

Use of the data in publications is encouraged, but subject to the following conditions:

  • The observers concerned should be contacted to confirm the particular acquisition and data reduction procedures used.
  • The campaign PI should be contacted to determine if the planned analysis could be a collaborative effort or an additional analysis.
  • Observers contributing data or whose data are useful to the findings of the paper should be invited to be co-authors.
  • The following acknowledgement should be included in all publications: This publication is based (in part) on spectroscopic data obtained through the collaborative Southern Astro Spectroscopy Email Ring (SASER) group.
